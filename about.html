Our history
Since 1964

Born in Ladner. Proven on the Pacific.

More than 60 years ago, a commercial crab fisherman in Ladner started building traps for his own boat. Other fishermen noticed how well they worked. Before long, the traps were being built for local crews, and the name Ladner Trap became known on the water.

That origin still defines the company: practical gear built to fish hard, handle cleanly, and last.

60+
Years of trap-building experience

Trusted from California through BC and into Alaska.

From one fisherman’s boat to a West Coast name.

The first Ladner traps were built to fish, not to show. The design earned its reputation through saltwater, heavy use, and commercial fishermen who depend on their gear.

As demand grew, the line expanded into prawn, shrimp, black cod, zincs, bait, and rigging. The focus stayed the same: durable commercial gear.

1984 and forward.

In 1984, new ownership expanded Ladner Traps into a broader range of commercial trap products.

Today, Ladner Traps is operated by Sealtech Manufacturing Inc. from Delta, BC, carrying forward the same principle: build quality gear that helps fishermen fish productively.
